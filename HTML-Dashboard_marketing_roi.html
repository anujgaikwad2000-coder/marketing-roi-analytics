Insight 01
Email campaigns deliver 15,137% ROI — generating $261M on just $1.7M spend. Massively underfunded relative to potential.
Insight 02
31.3% of total budget ($10.2M) is spent on negative-ROI campaigns — recoverable if reallocated to Email, Affiliate, or SEO channels.
Insight 03
Influencer campaigns consume 36.3% of spend but return only 103% ROI — the lowest efficiency. Average CAC exceeds $450 vs $45 for Email.
Insight 04
North America drives 35% of revenue but has the highest per-campaign cost. Asia Pacific has 22% lower CAC — a high-growth market being under-invested.
Insight 05
Q4 (Oct–Dec) generates 28% more revenue than Q1 due to seasonal demand. Current budget allocation does not reflect this peak period.
Insight 06
90.5% of impressions never result in a click. A CTR improvement from 9.5% to 12.5% would yield an estimated +$22M in incremental revenue annually.
Insight 07
Top 25% of campaigns by ROI generate 62% of total net profit — Pareto principle in action. Scale winners; eliminate the bottom quartile.
Insight 08
Affiliate channels show 12.9% CVR — highest of all types — yet receive only 7.2% of total budget. Strong case for reallocation.
Insight 09
Paid Ads drive $103M revenue at $9M cost — 1,045% ROI. High absolute value but 15× lower efficiency than Email. Targeting refinement needed.
Insight 10
SEO/Content campaigns compound over time (3,368% ROI) with the lowest decay rate — budget here builds durable organic traffic rather than one-time spikes.
